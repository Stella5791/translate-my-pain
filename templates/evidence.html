Evidence & Sources

About this Tool

This tagging system draws on research into the language of pain in endometriosis and chronic pelvic conditions, led by Dr Stella Bullo. It focuses on how patients use metaphor to express complex, embodied experiences that are often under-acknowledged in clinical communication.

Core Research

  • Bullo, S. (2024). The Language of Endometriosis. In preparation. [Empirical data on metaphor use]
  • Bullo, S. (2023). I feel like I’m being stabbed… Paper presented at the Discourse & Health Conference, Manchester.
  • Bullo, S. (2024). Parallel Words: Patient Language in Endometriosis. British Journal of Health Psychology.
