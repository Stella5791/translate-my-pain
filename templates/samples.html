Research-Based Sample Expressions

Pain is a subjective and complex experience. Many people find that it's easier to explain their pain by comparing it to something else — a metaphor. Research shows that people often use vivid comparisons to describe menstrual and pelvic pain, especially in conditions like endometriosis.

Think about how your pain feels during different moments — like ovulation, menstruation, intercourse, bowel movements, or even when nothing seems to be triggering it. Does your pain feel overwhelming, violent, suffocating, or something else entirely? And how does it make you feel emotionally?
